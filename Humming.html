HummingBird

A virtual reality theater project is an immersive experience that combines the technology with the concept of a theater. This virtual reality theater, some of the audience wear a VR headset that covers their eyes, creating a 3D visual experience. It allows the audience to step into a virtual environment where they can watch the show or interactive in the show. This immersive experience adds a new level of engagement and realism to the viewing experience.

My role as part of the VR Construct Contributor. My duty is to render object into 3D, as well the background of the scene and make them be interactive. In order to be interactive, I had to make sure the C# code is correct.

DES 318 Typography III: Expression

Textual investigations of typographic form of expression with an emphasis on composition (contrast, rhythm, tension), non-linearity, visual argumentation, dimensions, and the interplay between digital and analog operations.

Assignment1

For this assignment, I had to select or create a phrase, slogan or quote that must be between 3-10 words. Then, develope typographic compositions using the chosen phrase and design. Focus on the design and the content and not so much on technical perfection. I like this assignment because I could express and expierment with typo and colors. I had use photography, a little of handwritten, and some typography.
